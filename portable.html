📦 Portability and Efficiency

The Echo Engine Portable is the most compact and economical version of the line. Designed to be discreet and easy to transport, it measures only 24.8 × 14.3 × 3 mm — ideal for environments where space and mobility are essential.

🔋 Autonomy and Energy

  • Powered by rechargeable lithium battery
  • Compatible with common cell phone chargers
  • Up to 72 hours of activity with a single charge

💡 Features

Despite the reduced size, the Portable model maintains the main features of the Echo Engine Basic:

  • Local visual feedback
  • Storage of the last 24 hours
  • Wi-Fi connection: client mode or access point
  • Web interface for configuration and monitoring
  • Remote update via OTA

🔧 Hardware Used

  • Microcontroller: ESP8266 ESP-01
  • Low-cost sound sensors
  • Lithium battery with protection circuit

🌍 Applications

Ideal for use in educational, residential or urban environments, where sound monitoring needs to be discreet, accessible and reliable.
