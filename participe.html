💡 Why participate?

The Echo Engine project was born from a desire to make the world more inclusive. Each contribution helps to:

  • 💻 Develop new prototypes with sensors and microcontrollers
  • 🧪 Test devices in real-world environments
  • 📦 Create educational kits for schools and NGOs
  • 🌐 Maintain the project's digital infrastructure
  • 📚 Produce tutorials and training materials

🚀 How can you participate?

🔍 Transparency

All funds raised will be invested directly in the development and distribution of the devices. Progress reports will be available for anyone who wishes to follow along.
